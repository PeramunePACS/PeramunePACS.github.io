    SLIIT - 4th Year Research Project

    Interactive Chemistry Learning Assistant

    This study explores a mobile application which can be of use to assist students to achieve higher levels of brain productivity by analyzing their intellectual level along with the General Certificate of Education Advanced Level in Sri Lanka math and science fields.

    Project Overview

    This study explores a mobile application which can be of use to assist students to achieve higher levels of brain productivity. Students' intellectual levels are analyzed using reinforcement learning technic. Teachers can use the enthusiasm and imaginative thinking that comes with utilizing technology in the classroom to help students perform better. Augmented Reality within touch gestures, gamification dashboard and score system have been used to create the solution. Most students will achieve to improve their chemistry skills in sustainability, teamwork, solidarity, innovation, creativity, problem-solving, continuous improvement, energy efficiency, mathematical precision, initiative, goal achievement, result orientation, flexibility, and working with the environment.

    User Intellectual Analyzing

    Develop a questionnaire to measure the intelligence level of students.Then can decide the students' behaviours and knowledge of the subject.

    Visualize concepts using AR

    Integrate AR objects together using fingertip.3D objects, subject materials are Visualizing Planes & Placing Objects.

    UI/UX Intergration

    Creating interfaces to present subject content, questions, and solutions in a way that students can catch quickly and in an engaging way.

    Score System

    Create a simple score system that can go too far in the game and get levels and achieve all the knowledge of chemistry.

    Research Project Scope

    Literature Survey
    • An augmented reality-based educational experience can turn the entire educational process into fun and entertainment.
    • Developing an AR mobile game-based application allows all users of the technology to embrace it for the improvement of learning.
    • Most of the analyzed research have not considered users' interactive designs and included Vuforia marks, to train and test AR-relevant models.
    Reaseach Gap
    • Touch gestures system to integrate each AR object together.
    • Visualizing Planes & Placing Objects.
    • In the current approach, a smaller number of students interacted with the science subject.
    • As a reason design user interface more interactively.
    • Lack of research in mobile game industry.
    Research Problem
    • How to keep the student engage in the game play without discouraging them?
    • Can a UI be used to encourage or to discourage in education?
    • By game level, how should the user approach the supplied AR basic task?
    Research Objectives
    • Create an interactive learning platform.
    • Mobile based application
    • learning process must integrate into a mobile application
    Methodology
    • Detect user’s intellectual analysis system for level.
    • Visualize Augmented Reality within touch gesture.
    • UX qualification-based dashboard.
    • Unique Score System.

    MILESTONES

    • Project Proposal

      (6th September 2021 - Report - 6%, Presentation 6%)

      The main interaction between the client and the product creator is the project proposal. It explains what the system does, how much it costs, and the first actions to take after it's built. The interview panel is made up of three intellectuals who are experts in subjects that are related to the proposed system's functionality.

    • Progress Presentation-1

      (10th January 2022 - Presentation 15%)

      The position where the system is at 50% complete is known as the progress presentation 01 or Prototype presentation. Individuals' essential functions should be operational, and the entire system is not required.

    • Progress Presentation-2

      (4th May 2022 - Presentation 18%)

      The situation in which the overall structure should be operating at 90% efficiency is referred to as progress presentation number two. The capabilities should operate together so that the final product decisions may be recognized by running the system from beginning to finish.

    • Final Presentation

      (6th July 2022 - Presentation 10%)

      The final presentation occurs when the system is completely functional and ready to go. That is to say, the system must be a complete product that can be delivered.

    • Viva

      (6th July 2022 - Presentation 10%)

      Individual assessments of each group member to determine their degree of comprehension of the system and its activities.
